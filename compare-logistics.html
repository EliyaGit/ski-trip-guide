What’s included

  • Flights (economy): SF group → GVA/MXP; NY group → GVA/MXP/LYS.
  • Transfers: private/shared shuttle or train+funicular where it’s handy.
  • Lodging: mid-to-nice slope-side hotels or Arc 1950 residences (double occupancy).
  • Lift passes: 6-day adult.
  • Food & drink: breakfast, on-mountain lunches, dinners with drinks.
  • Extras: rental/demo skis for those not bringing gear, spa time.
